About Us

Fresh baked goods and Banh Mi in San Jose

Our History

Established in 1992.
111 Bakery is a mom and pop shop business, for nearly 30 years our small business has been serving the people of North Side San Jose with the utmost respect by providing them with quick and attentive service and an assortment of fresh baked goods as well as banh mi sandwiches that are made with love and dedication. Through these years, we have seen many faces and made lots of memories with our beloved customers through our baking and bread making skills.

Our Specialities

Our business specializes in a variety of baked goods. We are most notably known for our banh mi bread which are baked every morning. Our delectable sandwiches comes in a variety of fillings (meat/ vegetarian) which are always made to order using the freshest ingredients. We also have an assortment of buns and pastries that will satisfy anyone with a sweet or savory craving.
