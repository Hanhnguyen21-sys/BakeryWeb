Our Menu

Must Try Items

Triple One bakery is well-known for Vietnamese sandwiches and assortments of buns and pastries. We sell banh mi with different fillings from marinated meats, such as grilled pork, or grilled chicken, to vegetarian options. Currently, we also have new variations of American sandwiches. All sandwiches are served with our airy and crunchy baguettes. Besides, our store sells a wide rage of freshly baked buns and pastries, including savory breads like Ham and Cheese to sweet pastries such as Cheese Danish.

Meatball

Banh Mi

Our Vietnamese sandwiches are served with fresh baked, crispy, and fluffy banh mi (also known as baguette). They all come with a base of mayo, which adds a juiciness and creamy to the banh mi. To add more flavors and textures, our sandwiches are stuffed with veggies, including pickled daikon and carrot, cucumber, cilantro, and jalapeno. Lastly, we finish the banh mi with a little drizzle of soy sauce and a pinch of black pepper to enhance savory richness.
